NetWell

Your health, compounded.

After deciding to take my health and finances seriously, I realized I needed a way to track both. NetWell helps me see the real impact of changing my habits—not just in how I feel, but in what I save. It tracks my fitness progress while also logging the money I don't spend on things like iced coffee and takeout. Every skipped temptation compounds into both better health and smarter financial decisions. Built with React Native and Apple Watch integration to make tracking effortless.
