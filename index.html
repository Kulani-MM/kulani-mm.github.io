About Me

hi, there! i'm kulani

I grew up in a small village — where routine was safety, and change felt risky. Naturally, I chose Accounting — it was the structured path, the responsible one. And it did teach me discipline and systems thinking.

But something in me needed movement. Accounting felt like watching the same day repeat — I wanted to create, not just record.

So after just two weeks back in an accounting job, I quit. It was terrifying — I was 27, with no guarantee that tech would welcome me. But I took that leap anyway and joined WeThinkCode_.

Here, I found my people — a community that thrived on curiosity and collaboration. I learned to code, but more importantly, I learned to think differently.
